Data

Tweet data comes from the The Trump Twitter Archive. The data was collected on the 13th of March, 2019, and spans the range 23/01/17 to 12/03/19. I filtered out tweets that began with a quote or "RT" so that I would keep only tweets containing Trump's own language.
